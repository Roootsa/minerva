My Biodata

Maulana Faizar Rasyadan was born in Serang on February 3 2004. I have a motto to always try to grow and develop. After graduating from school, I chose not to go straight to college. Instead, I developed and applied my knowledge by becoming a teacher and secretary at a modern Islamic boarding school in the city of Rangkasbitung. There, I developed various secretarial skills and also learned design using Corel Draw.

I really enjoy learning new things and feel proud when I successfully overcome various obstacles in life.
